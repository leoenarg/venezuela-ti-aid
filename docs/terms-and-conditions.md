# Terms and Conditions Draft

## Humanitarian Use Only

venezuela-ti-aid exists only to support humanitarian assistance, family reunification, and emergency location of missing or vulnerable people.

By using the platform, users agree not to misuse information, submit false reports, scrape data, harass people, expose vulnerable people, or use the service for political, commercial, discriminatory, violent, or unlawful purposes.

## User Responsibilities

Users submitting reports must:

- Provide information in good faith.
- Avoid publishing rumors as facts.
- Avoid uploading images or data they know are false or malicious.
- Respect the dignity and safety of missing, found, deceased, injured, and minor persons.
- Understand that reports are community-submitted and may be incomplete or incorrect.

## No Emergency Guarantee

The app is not a police, medical, rescue, or emergency dispatch service. It does not replace official emergency channels, hospitals, shelters, civil protection, humanitarian organizations, or legal authorities.

## Privacy and Access Limits

The platform intentionally does not provide a public directory. Search requires exact identity verification through cedula and date of birth.

## Prohibited Uses

Forbidden uses include:

- Mass scraping or data extraction.
- Publishing lists of people from the system.
- Political targeting or persecution.
- Extortion, threats, harassment, or doxxing.
- Commercial use or sale of information.
- Facial recognition, biometric matching, or automated identification.
- Uploading false, defamatory, or intentionally harmful reports.
- Attempts to bypass privacy restrictions or Row Level Security.

## Infrastructure Providers

The application may rely on third-party infrastructure providers such as Vercel, Supabase, and GitHub. These providers are not authorized by the project to misuse user data, but they may process data as necessary to host, store, secure, and operate the service under their own legal terms.

## Removal and Correction

Before production, the operator must publish a contact channel for correction, deletion, takedown, and abuse reports.

## Legal Review

These terms are a starting draft for humanitarian software governance. They must be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before production use.
